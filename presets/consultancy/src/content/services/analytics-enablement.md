## The short answer

This service makes the website easier to learn from.

## What it usually solves

Without clean instrumentation, teams argue about performance using partial evidence. That slows optimization and makes content or campaign decisions harder than they need to be.

## How the work is approached

Start by defining what decisions the business wants to make. Then work backward into the events, forms, conversions, and reporting structure needed to support those decisions.
