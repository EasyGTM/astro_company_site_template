# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and clear.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or moderate comments, issues, or contributions that violate these expectations.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method listed in `SECURITY.md` or the repository profile.
