## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how **Skynet LLC** ("we", "us", or "our") may collect, use, and protect personal information when someone visits our website, submits a contact form, or engages with our services.

## Information we may collect

We may collect:

- contact details such as name, email address, company name, and phone number
- project information submitted through forms
- analytics and device information such as browser type, pages viewed, and referral source
- communication history when you email us or request support

## How we use information

We may use personal information to:

- respond to enquiries
- deliver requested services
- improve site performance and content
- understand which pages and resources are useful
- meet legal or operational obligations

## Form submissions

This template supports a configurable REST form endpoint. If you connect a third-party API, you are responsible for documenting that processing activity and making sure it complies with your applicable privacy obligations.

## Cookies and analytics

You may choose to use analytics, cookies, or advertising tools on top of this template. If you do, update this policy to explain what is collected, why it is collected, and how users can manage their preferences.

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers that help us operate the website or deliver services, subject to appropriate contractual and security safeguards.

## Data retention

We keep information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures to protect information, but no website or transmission method is completely secure.

## Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, objection, portability, or restriction of your personal information.
