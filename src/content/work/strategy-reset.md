## Challenge

The firm had strong expertise but no stable message. The homepage, proposals, and sales decks all described the work differently, which made every new asset a fresh debate.

## Approach

The engagement focused on narrowing the category language first, then rolling that language into service-page structure, proof blocks, and conversion calls to action.

## Outcome

The new site gave the team a reusable narrative system instead of one polished homepage and a collection of unresolved downstream pages.
