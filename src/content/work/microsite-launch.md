## Challenge

The client needed to enter a new regional market quickly without overhauling the full corporate site.

## Approach

We treated the microsite as a focused conversion path rather than a smaller copy of the main site. Messaging, offer structure, and landing pages were all optimized for the launch audience.

## Outcome

The client ended up with a launch asset that could be reused, not just a one-off page set that expired after the campaign ended.
