The strongest process pages make the engagement feel concrete. They replace vague claims with a sequence of understandable steps and expected outputs.

## 1. Diagnose

Start with the current reality. Review positioning, traffic sources, conversion paths, proof assets, and technical constraints before deciding what needs to change.

## 2. Shape

Translate the findings into a practical plan: page hierarchy, offers, messaging, proof, content priorities, and the systems needed to support measurement.

## 3. Publish

Launch the revised experience with clean metadata, structured content, and fast front-end implementation. Make sure the team can keep editing the site after launch.

## 4. Improve

Use analytics, qualitative feedback, and search or AI discovery signals to decide what to refine next. The site should improve as questions and opportunities become clearer.

## Why this matters for AEO and SEO

Process pages often rank for service evaluation queries because they explain how the work is performed, not just what the offer is called. They also help AI tools produce more accurate summaries of how your company operates.

## Recommended edits

- add your actual timelines and review cadence
- swap the generic deliverables for the ones you truly provide
- mention tools or communication rhythms only if they matter to clients
- keep headings direct so the page stays easy to parse
